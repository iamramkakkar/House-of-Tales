TIME TOUR SOUL

Time flies over us, but leaves its shadow behind.

A time to remember, a time to forget. A time to sow, a time to reap. A time to think and a time to act. A time to travel and a time to create memories. Time is the factor that decides everything for us. Time is your most valuable asset; don't waste it. But how many of us really pay attention to life that’s rushed by us even as we review over what has to be done next?

If there is one thing in life you can never get back, it is time.

In today’s times too, what is considered attractive in one culture may not be considered so in another. Various experiments have been done over the years where one image was sent to designers across the world, asking them to make the girl look ‘beautiful’. What came out as a result of this experiment was interesting. And that is – There is no universal concept of beauty.

Whenever you have some free time, you might start doing things such as watching TV programs you have no interest in or daydreaming: this is what I call wasting time.

We are so busy in pursuing are goals, ambitions and reaching the destination that we forget that the journey of life is lost in the deals. And life just ends up with the series of IF only…. but then…. Why me filled with regrets and missed opportunities. We are so busy judging between what appears right and wrong that we forget to live and time is rushing.

Problems at life will come and go. The choice to indulge in self pity or to get up and face life as it comes is the decision that is simply ours.

Life teaches us to make good use of time, while time teaches us the value of life. Get busy living your life. You have just one good chance at life.

Where is time to hate when there is so little time to love? so live, laugh and love. Life is all about that moment that we spent carefree, a moment that we spent peacefully with our loved ones, that one moment where we laughed and giggled over the silliest jokes or pranks with friends and colleagues.

Time is precious, don’t waste it !
Go! travel the world , explore it , complete your dreams , create memories, enjoy your company in that moment and time.
